Solar and Smart Energy Systems

February 27- 10:00 am to February 28- 5:00 pm
Department of Physics, KU, Vidyaranyapuri + Google Map

Registration Fee:

* For any single day workshop per participant the amount is Rs.800/-

* For a two day workshop per participant the amount is Rs.1500/-

* For any single day workshop the amount per group is Rs.4000/- and for a two day workshop it is Rs.7500/-(A group should consist of 5 members and a workshop kit is given to each group).

Description:

This workshop involoves hands-on-experience on two energing fields-Renewable and Electrical energy efficiency.

On first day, participants learn about renewable energy resources and techniques to yield electrical energy using solar power.Thus includes practical exposure to solar production along with real-life application of charging mobile devices.

The second day starts with the concepts on smart energy and other industrial trends like green energy, smart building, smart grid and energy harvesting technologies. To help participants learn about efficient use of electrial energy a prototype smart traffic control system is executed as project outcome

Details:

It is a 2 days project.On first day we conduct project on solar mobile charging, second day will be on smart traffic method.

Overview:

* Renewable energy

* Solar panel-Functioning & Application

* Smart energy systems

* Trafficcontrolled systems, Design and Implementation
