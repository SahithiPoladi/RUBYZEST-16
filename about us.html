university college of engineering and technology was established in 2013 by professor B.Venkat Rathnam(vc in 2013) and Dr.T.Sumathi Uma Maheshwari bearing principle of ku womens. This is the first women's engineering college among all Telangana universities. Our main vision and mission To be the leading centre of under graduation for women. To excel in teaching and research through creativity and productivity. To inculcate competitive spirit among students with a global vision. To strive constantly to achieve high standards in teaching and research. To carve out a niche for the University imparting higher quality teaching with a holistic approach. To encourage interdisciplinary teaching and research. To conduct collaborative academic and research programmes. To design, publish and disseminate relevant literature to strengthen higher education, social and cultural integration.
